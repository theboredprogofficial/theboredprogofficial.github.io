this is not a company in the traditional sense.
There's no CEO.
No board.
No investors.
There's a guy with CAD, a 3D printer, and an obsession with tolerances.

I release files, you print them.
Want the real thing? — my print, my assembly, my packaging — buy the kit.

Why open source?

Because I've had too many tools die from a broken plastic clip.
Because I want you to fix your own shit.
Because if I get hit by a bus tomorrow, the support doesn't cease to exist.

What's next?

Whatever I need next. A stand for my laptop, a speaker enclosure, a coffee tool; I don't plan, I just make.
